Sound of
the Universe

2018 -

Synesthetes can see essence, so it could be that it is possible to see sounds and any person can have a sound of itself.

This project started from my interest to hear the “inner sound” of musicians and has developed into the idea of connection. For now the project includes three different parts, which contains portraits and pictures of landscapes.

My idea is that sound the same as air is around us so we connected by it with the same approach we connected by air. We need to take care of it, when we’re talking or singing we’re breathing deeper. The same as air, we can't ignore sounds around us.

With this project I’m also looking for an opportunity for visually impaired and blind people to hear photography instead of see it and have more experience during exhibition.
