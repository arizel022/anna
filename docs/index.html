Hanna Melnykova

Photographer, director, curator.

Born in Ukraine.

Her work has been exhibited across Europe and Ukraine, and her book “Largo” dedicated to the study of gender in modern society was presented at the UNSEEN festival in Amsterdam.

Anna’s photographs stored in private collections in different countries from Ukraine to USA.

For the past five years she has taught master classes and given workshops across Europe.
