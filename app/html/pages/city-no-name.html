City No name

City No name

2020 - 2022

The image of the environment in which we live is determined by the fantasies of a person who creates a new landscape. In the new reality, the magical atmosphere of the landscape collides with the rigidity of the geometric volumes of sprawling cities. The accelerating rhythm of life absorbs and depersonalizes a person. We are lost in the conditions that we have created ourselves. We become just a silhouette, in an elusive way.

Made in analog print technique, this project is an experiment and an attempt to depict a new reality and a portrait of a new landscape. There are different cities and countries in which I have ever had to live that are connected in one image. Carefully photographed in intimate settings, the portraits are almost completely rendered in the printing process to show how big cities can make a person invisible.
